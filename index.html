Before you start

You will need

  • White fabric or plain white paperFor the white shirt test. Hold it close to the neckline.
  • Cream or ivory fabric, paper, or clothingUse it to confirm whether warmth looks richer or washed out.
  • Gold and silver metalJewelry, watch bands, buttons, or hardware work. Avoid gemstones for this test.
  • Indirect daylight and a mirrorFace a window. Avoid yellow lamps and harsh bathroom light.
